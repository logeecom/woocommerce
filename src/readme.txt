= ChannelEngine - your multichannel ecommerce platform =

Expanding your ecommerce by selling on online marketplaces can be a strong driver for revenue-growth. These platforms grant access to a broad customer base that may not discover your online store otherwise.

ChannelEngine provides a user-friendly extension connecting your WooCommerce store to hundreds of marketplaces and online sales channels worldwide, including Amazon, Walmart, Zalando, Bol, Lazada, and many more.

Our platform consistently adds new marketplaces each month, ensuring comprehensive coverage across different industries and regions. Start selling your products directly from your WooCommerce store to reach more customers and unlock additional revenue streams.

= Control your multichannel sales from one central hub =

ChannelEngine facilitates seamless management of your multichannel sales by establishing a direct connection with WooCommerce and various online marketplaces, acting as the centralized hub for your ecommerce operations. Key features include:
* Streamlined listings: Effortlessly upload products from your Woo catalog and select the marketplaces you want to sell on.
* Inventory management: Automatically synchronize inventory levels between your WooCommerce store and the marketplaces you engage with.
* Pricing and promotions: Tailor and optimize your offers and pricing on a per-item andper-channel basis.
* Efficient order handling: Ensure a seamless customer experience with efficient order management.
* Shipping management: Our system updates the shipping status to each specificmarketplace, keeping customers informed about the order's progress.
* Cancellations: In the event of marketplace order cancellations within WooCommerce, ChannelEngine swiftly updates the respective marketplace.

= Expand your ecommerce with ease =

Our platform is designed to help grow your ecommerce across marketplaces. Discover everything it has to offer:
* Effortless integration: Link your WooCommerce store to various marketplaces using our extension, bootstrapping your multi-channel sales strategy.
* Broadened reach: Explore an extensive network of 700+ marketplaces and online sales channels, connecting you with millions of potential customers beyond the confines of your Woocommerce store.
* Centralized control: Keep your product information, pricing, and inventory up to date across multiple marketplaces from one intuitive interface.
* Enhanced visibility: Elevate your brand exposure by presenting your products on popular marketplaces, increasing the chances of sales and enhancing brand recognition.
* Time and cost efficiency: Manage all your marketplace sales directly from your WooCommerce environment, eliminating the need for manual listing and constant inventory updates.
* Expert guidance: Access the expertise of ChannelEngine's marketplace professionals, ready to assist with any integration or marketplace-related queries, ensuring a seamless and reliable selling experience.
